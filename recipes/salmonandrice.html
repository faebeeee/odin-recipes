Salmon and Rice

Description

Recipe for a healthy and tasty meal, perfect for all fish lovers that are looking to hit their protein goal!

Ingredients

  • 300g salmon filet
  • 100g dry basmati rice
  • 2TS olive oil
  • Your favourite seasonings
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1/2 lemon

Steps

  1. Season the salmon with the lemon and your seasonings
  2. Preheat your pan with oil
  3. In a bigger pan start cooking your garlic in oil
  4. Add the rice to the pan cooking with garlic
  5. Add water to the rice and let it cook for 10min
  6. Place salmon in preheated pan and cook on both sides for about 3min until golden brown

Kaiserschmarn

Description

Recipe for a healthy version of the world famous austrian Dish Kaiserschmarn.

Ingredients

  • 2TL Baking powder
  • 2TL Honey
  • 80g Spelled Flour
  • 160g Skyr
  • 3 Eggs

Steps

  1. Carefully seperate the egg white from the yolk
  2. Beat egg whites until stiff
  3. Put the yolk, flour, honey, skyr and baking powder into one cup and mix thouroughly
  4. Stir in the stiff egg white with the rest
  5. Place in pan and cook until golden brown

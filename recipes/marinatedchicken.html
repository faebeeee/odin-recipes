Marinated Chicken

Description

This marinated chicken is perfect for every BBQ you might want to attend. It is easy to prepare and most importantly very healthy

Ingredients

  • 300g chicken
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 2TS soy sauce
  • 1TS of honey
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put the chicken into a big bowl
  2. Press clove of garlic and lemon into the same bowl
  3. Add the soy sauce and the honey
  4. Season to your liking
  5. Mix thouroughly
  6. Let marinate in fridge for at least one hour
  7. Grill and enjoy
